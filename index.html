About Me

Originally from Ethiopia, eastern part of Africa, I moved to Nashville with my family a few years ago. After completing my degree in Business Management in 2008, I founded and subsequently served as a principal of a pre and elementary school for nine years before moving with my family to the beautiful city of Nashville. After settling in Nashville, I worked in various capacities, including operations support, quality assurance, and business intelligence/analytics. Over the past three years, I have been working as a Business Intelligence Analyst, leading and supporting data analysis and reporting efforts to support decision-making. This experience has not only given me the opportunity to learn about analytics tools and technologies, but it has also helped further develop my analytical and strategic thinking skills. Driven by desire to grow and expand my problem solving and creativity skills, I made the decision to get out of my comfort zone and join Nashville Software School. Balancing the demands of school and full-time employment, while raising a young family has not been easy. Through this journey I have learnt to prioritize and effectively manage my time, as well as to deal with different types of challenges. With every single obstacle or challenge, I see myself growing and moving one step closer to my goal. I am still a work in progress. I want to see myself transform from mediocre to magnificent. I am learning to thrive with these challenges, and realizing this is how I learn best.
